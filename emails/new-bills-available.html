Why do my current charges differ from my account balance?

The number shown in the Current Charges column represents only the Current charges and associated taxes for the selected bill period.

The Account balance column represents the current charges for the selected bill period plus/minus other adjustments. This could include carry-over balances from previous bills, late payment charges, or other miscellaneous adjustments applied to the account.
